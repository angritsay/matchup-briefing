HOW TO PLAY IT

levels 1–3 decide this lane · «3 minutes of mistakes = 25 minutes of suffering»
1

Play off your wave NO BUSH CONTEST

He wins level-1 bush fights. If he wards your bush at 1:20 he's fishing for Q1 on your facecheck. Stack Q resets, poke through minions only.

2

Race to the 7th minion YOUR WINDOW

Your E gets you to level 2 first. Force one short trade: E-stun → Q → out before his next chain starts.

3

Dodge Q2 · punish after Q3 SKILL CHECK

Hold your dash for his second Q, eat the empowered auto with W. After Q3: ~7s of nothing — full trade with resets.
